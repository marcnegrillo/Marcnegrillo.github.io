⚡ n8n Lead Capture Workflow
Case Study

Lead Capture & Data Pipeline

An automated intake system that captures form submissions in real time, sends instant confirmations, cleans the data, and stores leads directly to Google Sheets — without a single manual step.

📥 Trigger: Form Submission 🔗 Integrations: n8n Forms, Gmail, Google Sheets ⚡ Latency: Real-time 💼 Use Case: Sales & Lead Gen
⚠ Challenge

Sales teams were manually checking inboxes for new inquiries, copy-pasting contact details into spreadsheets, and often missing leads entirely — costing opportunities and wasting hours each week.

✓ Solution

A four-node n8n workflow that automatically captures every submission, confirms receipt instantly, normalizes field data, and writes it to a shared Google Sheet in under two seconds.

⚡ Workflow Process
📋 Form Submitted→ 📩 Confirmation Sent→ 🔧 Data Cleaned→ 📊 Sheet Updated
1

Form Trigger — The workflow fires the instant a visitor submits the lead form. n8n receives the raw payload and begins processing with zero delay.

2

Confirmation Message — An automated reply is sent immediately to the lead, confirming receipt and setting expectations — reducing drop-off and building trust from the first touchpoint.

3

Set Node (Data Cleaning) — Field values are normalized: names are trimmed, phone numbers are formatted, and empty fields are flagged — so the data arriving in the sheet is always clean and consistent.

4

Google Sheets Write — The prepared record is appended to the live spreadsheet in real time. The sales team sees new leads the moment they arrive, with all fields ready for follow-up.

Case Study

Monday.com → Google Chat

Real-time team notifications triggered by any change in a Monday.com project board — keeping the entire team informed of who changed what, when it happened, and which department it belongs to.

🔄 Trigger: Board Item Changed 🔗 Integrations: Monday.com, Google Chat 👥 Use Case: Team Collaboration ⚡ Latency: Instant
⚠ Challenge

Teams working across departments had no reliable way to know when project statuses, deadlines, or assignments changed in Monday.com — leading to miscommunication, duplicated work, and missed handoffs.

✓ Solution

A Make.com scenario that watches the Monday.com board for any item change and instantly formats and dispatches a rich notification to the team's Google Chat space with full context.

⬡ Workflow Process
📋 Board Change Detected→ 🔀 Make.com Routes→ ✍️ Message Formatted→ 💬 Chat Notified
1

Monday.com Watch Trigger — Make.com monitors the board continuously. Any change — status update, assignee swap, deadline shift, or department field edit — fires the scenario immediately.

2

Smart Routing & Filtering — Make.com picks up the change and routes it through smart workflow logic, ensuring only meaningful updates trigger notifications and avoiding alert fatigue.

3

Message Formatting — The change data is structured into a clean, readable notification: who made the change, what was changed, the timestamp, and the department — formatted for quick scanning in chat.

4

Google Chat Dispatch — The formatted message is posted to the connected Google Chat space. Every team member sees exactly what changed without checking Monday.com manually.

🔷 GoHighLevel Appointment Reminder Automation
Case Study

Appointment Reminder Sequence

An automated two-stage reminder system that fires after every booking — sending a first reminder ahead of the meeting and a final reminder one hour before start — eliminating no-shows without a single manual follow-up.

⏱ Trigger: Appointment Confirmed 🔗 Integrations: GHL Workflow, Email 💼 Use Case: No-Show Reduction 📧 Touchpoints: 2 Automated Emails
⚠ Challenge

Clients were frequently missing scheduled appointments because reminders relied on manual follow-up — which was inconsistent, time-consuming, and often skipped entirely during busy periods, leading to wasted time slots.

✓ Solution

A GoHighLevel workflow that auto-enrolls every confirmed booking and sends two timed reminder emails — completely hands-free. The sequence runs reliably for every client, every time.

🔷 Workflow Process
📅 Appointment Booked→ ⏱ Wait→ 📧 First Reminder→ ⏱ Wait→ 📧 Final Reminder→ ✅ Done
1

Appointment Booked Trigger — The workflow starts the moment a customer confirms their booking. Auto-enrollment means no contact is ever missed, regardless of volume.

2

First Reminder — After a timed wait condition, a reminder email is sent confirming the upcoming appointment date and time, giving the client enough notice to prepare and reschedule if needed.

3

Final Reminder — A second wait condition triggers a final email approximately one hour before the meeting starts, ensuring the client is ready and reducing last-minute no-shows.

4

Workflow Ends — Once both reminders are delivered, the automation completes cleanly with no further action required from the team.

🔷 GoHighLevel CRM Pipeline Automation
Case Study

Multi-Pipeline Client Journey Automation

A complete sales-to-onboarding automation that detects pipeline stage changes in real time and automatically routes every client through the correct journey — from qualified lead to active client — with zero manual task creation.

🔁 Trigger: Pipeline Stage Changed 🔗 Integrations: GHL CRM, Tasks, Tags 💼 Use Case: Full Client Lifecycle 🔀 Branches: 7 Stage Conditions
⚠ Challenge

Sales teams were manually updating CRM records, creating follow-up tasks, and tracking pipeline stages — leading to inconsistent client experiences, missed follow-ups, and no visibility into where each deal stood in the pipeline.

✓ Solution

A GoHighLevel workflow with 7-branch intelligent routing that fires on every stage change — automatically creating the right task, applying the correct tag, and logging every action in real time for full lifecycle visibility.

🔷 Workflow Process
🔁 Stage Changed→ 🔀 Branch Condition→ ✅ Task Created→ 🏷 Status Updated→ 📊 Logged
1

Pipeline Stage Detection — The workflow fires the moment a contact's stage changes inside the Client Journey Pipeline, instantly identifying where the client has been moved — no polling, no delay.

2

Intelligent Stage Routing — A condition node evaluates the current stage and routes the contact into one of seven branches. Every client receives actions that are precisely relevant to their current position in the journey.

3

Automated Task & Status Actions — Each branch creates the correct task (review, discovery call, proposal follow-up, onboarding, requirement collection, check-in, or lost lead review) and updates the contact's tag and pipeline status automatically.

4

Complete Execution Logging — Every action is recorded in real time. The team gets full visibility into client journey progress, performance auditing, and easy troubleshooting across the entire lifecycle.

7 Pipeline Stages Handled Automatically

📌 Qualified Lead → Review task created

📌 Discovery Call → Call task assigned

📌 Follow-Up Proposal → Proposal task created

📌 Client Onboarding → Onboarding workflow started

📌 Collect Requirements → Requirements task created

📌 Active Client → Check-in scheduled

📌 Lost Lead → Re-engagement logged
